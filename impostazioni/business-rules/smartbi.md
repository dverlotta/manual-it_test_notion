## AdvancedSearchFields

**Tipo:** Valori separati da pipe  
**Valore di default:** Timeframe\|Customers\|CustomerGroups\|Items\|ErpDocumentTypes  
**Valori:**

* Customer
* CustomerGroup
* CustomerGroups
* Customers
* ErpDocumentType
* ErpDocumentTypes
* Item
* Items
* Timeframe
